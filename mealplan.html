🧀 Breakfast A · Ricotta Eggs
Easy days only (Tue 4/28)
470 kcal 36g P 8g C 30g F
Ingredients
  • 3 largeeggs
  • 5gunsalted butter
  • 150gpart-skim ricotta
  • 1 cupcoffee (black)
Method
  1. Melt butter in non-stick pan over medium-low heat.
  2. Whisk eggs and pour in. Stir gently until just set.
  3. Remove from heat, fold in ricotta — residual heat warms it.
  4. Season with salt and pepper. Serve immediately.

🥣 Breakfast C · Oats (Lift Days)
Mon 4/27 · Thu 4/30 · Sun 5/3
490 kcal 44g P 35g C 20g F
Ingredients
  • 40grolled oats
  • 30gwhey protein (1 scoop)
  • 100gpart-skim ricotta
  • ½ tspcinnamon
  • 1 cupcoffee (black)
Method
  1. Cook oats with water or milk to desired consistency.
  2. Let cool slightly, stir in whey protein.
  3. Top with ricotta and cinnamon. Do not microwave whey.
⚡ Fuels the lift. Eat 60–90 min before training.
🍳 Breakfast D · Post Long-Run
Sun 4/26 · Sat 5/2 (after long run)
560 kcal 40g P 55g C 20g F
Ingredients

  • 50g cookedblack beans

Method

🏃 Replenishes glycogen post long run. Don't skip.

Rules of the Road — v5
1
Protein first. 220g target every day. If under 2,100 kcal AND hitting 220g, the plan works. Add 1 whey scoop anywhere if falling short.
2
Sleep is the bottleneck this week. 4 hard days requires 4 nights of ≥7h. Apr 25 was your first 7h+ since Apr 20. If you can't hit 7h, eat the upper end of the calorie range — don't cut.
3
Hydration: 4L/day this week. 4 hard days + low-carb = sodium/electrolyte loss. Take an electrolyte tab daily, especially on long-run days.
4
Tuesday is your only easy day. Don't add anything to it — it's the recovery valve for the whole week. Use it for sleep.
5
If a session is in trouble, priority order to drop: (a) Mon strides, (b) one upper-body lift, (c) Wed tempo. Never drop the long runs or Sun 5/3 lower body — those are your high-leverage stimulus.
6
Boredom check. 8 days of ground beef + chicken. If you hit the wall by Wed, swap one chicken dinner for salmon or eggs+veg — same macros, different taste, saves compliance.
